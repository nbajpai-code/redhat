# Video Courses & Training

## Red Hat Certified System Administrator (RHCSA) - EX200

### Top Instructors & Platforms

#### Sander van Vugt (Highly Recommended)
Sander van Vugt is widely considered the gold standard for Red Hat certification training.
*   **[Red Hat Certified System Administrator (RHCSA) RHEL 9 Complete Video Course](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/red-hat-certified/9780137931535/)** (Available on O'Reilly / Pearson IT Certification)
*   **[Red Hat RHCSA 9 Cert Guide: EX200](https://www.amazon.com/Red-Hat-RHCSA-Cert-Guide/dp/0138221443)** (Book + practice tests)

#### Udemy Courses
Udemy offers affordable options, often with frequent sales. Be sure to check the reviews for recent updates regarding RHEL 9/10 content.
*   **Red Hat Certified System Administrator (RHCSA) Exam Prep** - Browse top-rated courses. Look for instructors who update frequently.
*   **Command Line & Scripting focused courses** - Useful supplements if you are weak in bash.

#### Pluralsight
*   **[RHCSA (EX200) Path](https://www.pluralsight.com/paths/red-hat-certified-system-administrator-rhcsa-ex200)** - A comprehensive learning path covering all exam objectives. Good for visual learners who already have a subscription.

## Red Hat Certified Engineer (RHCE) - EX294

### Top Instructors & Platforms

#### Sander van Vugt (Highly Recommended)
*   **[Red Hat Certified Engineer (RHCE) EX294 RHEL 9 Complete Video Course](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/red-hat-certified/9780136868276/)** - Covers all automation topics with Ansible.
*   **[Red Hat RHCE 8 (EX294) Cert Guide](https://www.pearsonitcertification.com/store/red-hat-rhce-8-ex294-cert-guide-9780136872938)** - Still valid for core concepts, check for RHEL 9 updates.

#### Udemy Courses (Ansible Focused)
Key courses for EX294 focusing on Ansible automation:
*   **[Red Hat Certified Engineer (RHCE) EX294: Complete Exam Prep (Uplatz)](https://www.udemy.com/course/red-hat-certified-engineer-rhce-ex294/)** - Updated content covering advanced Linux admin and automation.
*   **[Mastering RHCE EX294 - Linux Red Hat Certified Engineer (Muhammad Usman Anwar)](https://www.udemy.com/course/mastering-rhce-ex294/)** - High rating (4.7/5).
*   **[Red Hat Ansible Automation Training â€“ RHEL 8/9 (RH294)](https://www.udemy.com/course/red-hat-ansible-automation-training-rhel-8-rh294/)** - Comprehensive Ansible training.
*   **Prince Bajaj** - Known for realistic exam approaches.

#### CBT Nuggets
*   **[RHCE (EX294) Training](https://www.cbtnuggets.com/it-training/linux/rhce-ex294)** - "Very good" comprehensive course with labs.

### Strategy
1.  **Pick one main comprehensive course** (e.g., Sander van Vugt or a top-rated Udemy/CBT Nuggets course).
2.  **Supplement with hands-on labs**. Watching videos is not enough; you *must* write playbooks yourself.
3.  **Focus on Ansible Core**: Understand `ansible-navigator` vs `ansible-playbook` if using RHEL 9/AAP environments.
